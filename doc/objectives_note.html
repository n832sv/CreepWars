Upgrade your leader and fight for victory. Get started Go to Shop, upgrade your unit (it is advised to spend at least 50 gold on Hitpoints for beginners). After you've upgraded your hero, move to the center and fight. Each kill will increase gold and creep strength for your team. Human leaders cannot fully die, but if they get 'killed', they are teloperted home and spend 1 turn to restore. Killing enemy leaders is usually hard, but more rewarding (more on that below). To win the game, you must kill one of the enemy guards. Gold Each kill gives you 3 + 2*lvl gold: lvl0 lvl1 lvl2 lvl3 lvl4 3 5 7 9 11 gold you get for creep kill, depending on its level If you kill enemy leader, you get as much gold as your current creep score (rounded down). Creep generation Each team generates 8 creeps at the beginning of AI turn. If your creep score is below #creepwars_lvl0_barrier, only lvl0 units will be generated (randomly). If your creep score is #creepwars_lvl0_barrier..#creepwars_lvl3plus_barrier, you get units that cost as much as your creep score. If your creep score is #creepwars_lvl3plus_barrier+, a random lvl3 creep will be generated. If your creep score is more than 12.0 above this unit-s cost, the creep will be 'boosted' (+2 dmg, +1 strikes, +1 MP). Creep score Each kill gives your team some creep score. You can see the amount by hovering a unit (not implemented yet). Formula: (creep_cost ^ #creepwars_score_power) * #creepwars_score_scale. Killing enemy leader gives x2 of that amount.
