Get started Go to Shop. Choose how you want to upgrade your Leader, then move to the center and fight. Each kill will increase gold and creep strength for your team. Human leaders cannot fully die, but if they get 'killed', they are teloperted home and spend 1 turn to restore. Killing enemy leaders is usually hard, but more rewarding (more on that below). To win the game, you must kill one of the enemy guards. Gold Each kill gives you #creepwars_gold_for_lvl0 + #creepwars_gold_per_creep_level*lvl gold: lvl0 lvl1 lvl2 lvl3 lvl4 #lvl0g #lvl1g #lvl2g #lvl3g #lvl4g gold you get for creep kill, depending on its level If you kill enemy leader, you get gold equal to your current creep score (rounded down). Creep generation Each team generates 8 creeps at the beginning of AI turn. If your creep score is below #creepwars_lvl0_barrier, only lvl0 units will be generated (randomly). If your creep score is #creepwars_lvl0_barrier..#creepwars_lvl3plus_barrier, you get units that cost as much as your creep score. If your creep score is #creepwars_lvl3plus_barrier+, random lvl3 creeps will be generated. If your creep score is ahead of this unit cost by more than 12.0, the creep will receive 'boost' (+2 dmg, +1 strikes, +1 MP). Creep score Each kill gives your team some creep score. You can see the amount by hovering a unit (not implemented yet). Formula: (creep_cost ^ #creepwars_score_power) * #creepwars_score_scale. Killing enemy leader gives x2 of that amount.
