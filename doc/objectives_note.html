About This is a Human-vs-Human map, each team consists of 1-3 human leaders (2-3 recommended), and a minor AI side. To start the game, go to Shop, choose how you want to upgrade your Leader, then move to the center and fight. Each kill will increase gold and creep score for your team. If you lose all health, you are teloperted home and spend 1 turn to restore. Each AI side always generates units so that the total number of them is #creepwars_creep_count. Humans cannot recruit additional units, but they can spend their gold in Shop to buy upgrades. To win the game, you must kill one of the enemy guards. Gold Each creep kill gives gold. Hover a creep to see how much gold you'll get for killing it. Killing enemy leader gives you the value of your current creep score, in gold. Creep score Each kill gives your team some creep score. Creep generation Each AI side generates creeps for a total of #creepwars_creep_count at the beginning of its turn. The cost of chosen creeps is equal to your creep score. However, if your creep score is below #creepwars_lvl0_barrier, then only lvl0 units will be generated, and if your creep score is above #creepwars_lvl3plus_barrier, you get random lvl3 unit. If your score is much higher than unit cost, the unit will have additional 'boost'. Guard health Your guard health starts with #creepwars_guard_hp_initial and increases by #guard_creep_hp (configurable, default 1) when killing a creep and by 3x of that value when killing enemy leader. It is permanently decreased when the guard is attacked (legacy feature, might be removed). Development warning This is Development version, expect balancing breakage.
