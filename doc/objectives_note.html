About This is a Human-vs-Human map, each team consists of 1-3 human leaders (2-3 recommended), and a minor AI side. Each AI side always generates units so that the total number of them is #creepwars_creep_count. Humans cannot recruit additional units, but they can spend their gold in Shop to buy upgrades. To start the game, go to Shop, choose how you want to upgrade your Leader, then move to the center and fight. Each kill will increase gold and creep strength for your team. Human leaders cannot fully die: if they get 'killed', they are teloperted home and spend 1 turn to restore. To win the game, you must kill one of the enemy guards. Gold Each kill gives you #creepwars_gold_for_lvl0 + #creepwars_gold_per_creep_level*lvl gold: lvl0 lvl1 lvl2 lvl3 lvl4 #lvl0g #lvl1g #lvl2g #lvl3g #lvl4g gold you get for creep kill, depending on its level If you kill enemy leader, you get the value of your current creep score in gold, never above #creepwars_gold_for_leaderkill_max, rounded down. Creep score Each kill gives your team some creep score. You can see the amount by hovering a unit (not implemented yet). Formula: (creep_cost ^ #creepwars_score_power) * #creepwars_score_multiplier. Killing enemy leader gives x2 of that amount. Creep generation As said earlier, each AI side generates creeps for a total of #creepwars_creep_count at the beginning of its turn. The cost of chosen creeps is equal to your creep score. However, if your creep score is below #creepwars_lvl0_barrier, then only lvl0 units will be generated, and if your creep score is above #creepwars_lvl3plus_barrier, you get random lvl3 unit. If your score is much higher than unit cost, the unit will have additional 'boost'. Guard health Your guard health starts with #creepwars_guard_hp_initial and increases by #guard_creep_hp (configurable, default 1) when killing a creep and by 3x of that value when killing enemy leader. It is permanently decreased when the guard is attacked (legacy feature, might be removed). Development warning This is Development version, expect balancing breakage.
