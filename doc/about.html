About This is a Human-vs-Human map, each team consists of 1-3 human leaders (2-3 recommended), and a minor AI side. To start the game, go to Shop, choose how you want to upgrade your Leader, then move to the center and fight. To win the game, kill one of the enemy guards. Gold You get gold for each kill. First #gold_kills_to_increase kills give #gold_start_p0 gold, next #gold_kills_to_increase kills give #gold_start_p1 gold, next #gold_kills_to_increase kills kills give #gold_start_p2 gold, etc. Leader kill gives 4 times more gold, 4 kill count. Shop You Leader cannot recruit, but you can spend gold in Shop to buy upgrades. Visiting Shop will also heal your leader. Creep score Your creep score determines the cost of recruited AI creeps. You increase creep score with each kill. Only the number of killed units counts. Leader kill counts equally to creep kill. Creep generation The cost of new creeps is equal to your creep score. However, if your creep score is below #creepwars_lvl0_barrier, then only lvl0 units will be generated, and if your creep score is above #creepwars_lvl3plus_barrier, you get random lvl3 unit. If your score is much higher than unit cost, the unit will have additional 'boost'. Guard health Guard health increases by #guard_creep_hp (configurable, default 1) when killing a creep and by 3x of that value when killing enemy leader.
